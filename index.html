The question every board is asking

Are you ready for EU AI regulation?

The EU AI Act's general obligations apply from August 2026, with high-risk requirements phasing in through 2028. AI is the fastest-growing segment of German consulting — yet many companies still lack mature AI governance. If your AI touches customers, safety, or regulated data, the clock is already running.
