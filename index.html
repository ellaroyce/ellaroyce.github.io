Before you reach out

Frequently asked

How do we start?

With a free 20-minute call to hear where you are and what's on your mind. Most founders then book a first sparring session, a low-pressure way to see if we click before committing to anything ongoing.

Do you work remotely and across the EU?

Yes. I'm based in Germany and work with founders across Europe, remote by default, in person when it makes sense.

What's the difference between sparring and coaching?

Sparring is in the moment: you bring a live decision and we think it through together. Coaching is the longer game: regular sessions that keep you growing as a founder. Plenty of founders mix both.

What does the social media help cover?

Whatever level you need: shaping your personal brand, a simple content plan you can run yourself, or me handling the posting and management for you so it's off your plate.

How does pricing work?

We agree a simple scope up front, whether that's a single session, an ongoing slot, or social media support. No surprises and no hourly billing.
