What I do

Four things I sort out for you

Most companies come to me with one of these on fire and end up wanting all four. They are connected.

  1. 01

    Architecture

    A clear target picture for your systems, cloud and data, then a cleanup of what you have. Fewer tools, clear ownership, an architecture that scales with you instead of fighting you.

  2. 02

    Security

    Security sized to your business, not an enterprise checklist. I find where you are exposed, fix the urgent gaps first, and get you ready for ISO 27001 or NIS2 without the drama.

  3. 03

    Team

    Tools do not run themselves. I help you hire the right people, set up how IT works day to day, and coach the team to run it without me. Clear roles, sane processes.

  4. 04

    On-call leadership

    Senior judgement when you need it: a board deck to sanity-check, a vendor contract to read, a tough call to make. As much or as little as you need.

Frequently asked

How do we start?

A free intro call, then usually a half-day IT and security health check. You get a clear picture and a plan before committing to anything bigger.

Do you work remotely and across the EU?

Yes. I am based in Germany and work with companies across Europe, remote by default, on site when a project needs it.

Are you technical, or more of a strategist?

Both. I set the strategy for your board and get into the detail with your engineers. I do not hand you a slide deck and disappear.

We are small. Do we really need this?

If your tech or security is slowing you down or keeping you up at night, yes. You get senior judgement sized to where you are now, without a full-time hire.

How does pricing work?

A simple scope agreed up front: a one-off review, a fixed-scope project, or a few days a month. No surprises, no vague hourly billing.
