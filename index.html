What's usually on fire

Four modules, one system

Most founders come to me with one of these on fire and end up wanting all four. They're wired together, so I treat them as one system.

  1. 01

    Architecture

    A clear target picture for your systems, cloud and data, then a cleanup of what you have. Fewer tools, clear ownership, an architecture that scales with you instead of fighting you.

  2. 02

    Security

    Security sized to your business, not an enterprise checklist. I find where you are exposed, fix the urgent gaps first, and get you ready for ISO 27001 or NIS2 without the drama.

  3. 03

    Team

    Tools do not run themselves. I help you hire the right people, set up how IT works day to day, and coach the team to run it without me. Clear roles, sane processes.

  4. 04

    On-call leadership

    Senior judgement when you need it: a board deck to sanity-check, a vendor contract to read, a tough call to make. As much or as little as you need.
